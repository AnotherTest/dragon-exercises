== c)

S ::= 0 S 1 S | 1 S 0 S | 系

== d)

S ::= S~0~ | S~1~

S~0~ ::= S~c~ T~0~ S~0~ | T~0~ S~c~

T~0~ ::= 0 T~0~ | 0

S~1~ ::= S~c~ T~1~ S~1~ | T~1~ S~c~

T~0~ ::= 1 T~1~ | 1

== e)

It's easy to design a DFA for that by using Aho-Corasic automata and then convert it into context-free grammar.

The automata:

|=================================
State,Edge 0,Edge 1
0,1,0
1,1,2
2,1,None
|=================================

The grammar:

S~0~ = 0 S~1~ | 1 S~0~ | 系

S~1~ = 0 S~1~ | 1 S~2~ | 系

S~2~ = 0 S~1~ | 系
